# Conditions

## Blindness

- surprised on 1-4
- attack roll -4
- armor class -4
- initiative -2

## Deafness

- surprised on 1-3
- initiative -1

## Paralysis

- can't attack
- can't move
- can't take action

## Sleep

- can't attack
- can't move
- can't take action
- will end condition when attacked
